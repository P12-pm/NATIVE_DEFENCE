THE LIFECYCLE

Continuous Improvement Cycle

Awareness is not a one-time event. It's an ongoing culture.

STEP 01

Baseline Assessment

Action: Initial blind phishing test.

We send a simulated phishing email to establish the current "Phish-prone" percentage of your organization.

STEP 02

Interactive Training

Action: Content delivery.

Employees engage with gamified learning modules, videos, and quizzes tailored to their roles.

STEP 03

Ongoing Simulation

Action: Random testing.

Periodic, varied phishing and social engineering tests to keep security top-of-mind.

STEP 04

Reporting & Analytics

Action: Measure ROI.

Detailed executive reports showing the reduction in risk and improvement in employee reporting rates.

Course Content

Detailed Curriculum

What your team will learn.

  • Identifying spoofed domains and sender addresses.
  • Recognizing emotional triggers (Urgency, Fear, Greed).
  • Analyzing suspicious attachments and links safely.
  • Business Email Compromise (BEC) and CEO Fraud.

  • Understanding Vishing (Voice Phishing) and Smishing (SMS).
  • Physical security: Tailgating and shoulder surfing.
  • The dangers of oversharing on social media.
  • Verifying requests for sensitive data.

  • Risks of public Wi-Fi and using VPNs.
  • BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) policies.
  • Lost or stolen device protocols.
  • App permissions and data privacy.

  • Handling PII (Personally Identifiable Information).
  • Clean desk policy.
  • USB drive safety (Baiting attacks).
  • Incident reporting procedures.
